Guodong Du is currently a Second year Ph.D student in the Intelligent Multimedia Technology (IMT) Lab at the Xiamen University, under the supervision of Prof. Shaozi Li. He received the M.S. Degree in Computer Technique in 2018 from Kunming University of Science and Technology, Kunming, China.

His research interests include Data mining, machine learning and its applications in healthcare.
